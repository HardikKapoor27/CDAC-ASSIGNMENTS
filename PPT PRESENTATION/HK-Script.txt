🎞️ SLIDE 12 — Why Overcoming Stage Fear Matters
🎤 SCRIPT (1.5–2 min)

“What happens if we don’t overcome it?

First — we start missing opportunities.

Think about it…
presentations, interviews, group discussions…
these are all situations where speaking matters.

If we avoid speaking, we slowly start missing these chances.

Second — our ideas stay unheard.

You might have great knowledge, great thoughts…
but if you don’t express them, no one will ever know.

And over time… something else happens.

Our confidence starts decreasing.

Because the more we avoid speaking…
the more difficult it feels the next time.

It becomes a cycle.

But on the other hand—

Strong speaking skills can completely change your career.

It helps in interviews, leadership, teamwork… everything.

In fact, communication skills are considered one of the most important professional skills today.

So this is not just about speaking on stage…

It’s about expressing yourself in life.

Because at the end of the day…”

🎞️ SLIDE 13 — Key Takeaways
🎤 SCRIPT (1–1.2 min)

“Now let’s quickly summarize everything.

First — your brain is not your enemy.

Stage fear happens because of a natural fight-or-flight response…
your body thinks it’s in danger, even when it’s not.

Second — fear is normal.

Around 70–75% of people experience it,
so if you feel nervous… you’re not alone.

Third — nervousness is not always bad.

A little bit of nervousness can actually improve your focus and performance.

And finally —

Public speaking is not a talent… it’s a skill.

The more you practice, the more comfortable you become.

So don’t wait to feel confident…

Start speaking — and confidence will follow.”
